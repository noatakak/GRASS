The smeltItem function takes in bot, the itemName of what
you will be smelting, the fuelName, and the number of that
item that will be smelted. You must place a furnace before
calling this function. Example:  Smelt 1 raw_iron into
1 iron_ingot using 1 oak_planks as fuel:
smeltItem(bot, "raw_iron", "oak_planks");
